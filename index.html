About

Hi, I'm Priyanka.

I'm a digital marketer who fell hard for social. The trend that lands, the caption that gets a hundred replies, the launch that gets people talking. That's the fun part, and somehow it's also my job.

Over 6+ years I've grown brands across Web3, travel, crowdfunding, astrology, and startup media. I've managed ₹1Cr+ monthly budgets at Ketto, run £12K a month in Meta spend across UK travel, lifted a Web3 community's engagement 65% in three months, and stood on the ground at TOKEN2049 Dubai running the kiosk in real life, not just on a screen.

I blend creativity with data. I care about the numbers, but I care more about the people behind them. A follower count is just a number until those people actually feel something about your brand. That's the part I get right.
